Installers should be placed in corresponding directories 

JDK        - Download JDK from https://www.oracle.com/java/technologies/javase-downloads.html and place it in the jdk directory
WebLogic   - Download WebLogic server installer from https://www.oracle.com/middleware/technologies/weblogic-server-installers-downloads.html and place it in the wls directory 
deploytool - Download weblogic-deploy-tooling from https://github.com/oracle/weblogic-deploy-tooling/releases and place it in the deploytool directory
